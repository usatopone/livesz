Our Custom Solution Process

1

Consultation & Requirements Gathering

We begin with an in-depth consultation to understand your unique energy requirements, site constraints, and operational goals. Our engineers work with you to define the project scope, timeline, and budget.

  • Detailed energy load analysis
  • Site assessment (on-site or remote)
  • Review of technical and environmental challenges
  • Budget planning and cost optimization
  • Timeline establishment
2

Design & Concept Development

Our engineering team designs a custom solar plant concept. We provide detailed system diagrams, performance simulations, and equipment specifications for your review and feedback.

  • System modeling and energy simulation
  • Component selection and compatibility analysis
  • Structural and electrical engineering plans
  • Financial modeling (ROI, LCOE)
  • Design refinement iterations
3

Prototyping & Sampling

For highly unique projects, we can develop and test prototypes of key system components to validate performance and ensure the final design meets all expectations before full-scale deployment.

  • Component-level bench testing
  • Small-scale pilot system deployment
  • Performance data collection and analysis
  • Final design validation and approval
  • Final design approval process
4

Production & Quality Control

Once the design is approved, our certified technicians begin installation. We maintain strict quality control at every stage, with regular progress updates to keep you informed.

  • Procurement of specialized components
  • Professional installation and site management
  • Continuous quality assurance checks
  • System commissioning and final inspection
  • Grid interconnection and permitting
5

Delivery & Ongoing Support

After commissioning, we hand over the system and provide comprehensive training. Our post-installation support ensures your complete satisfaction and optimal system performance.

  • System handover and documentation
  • Operator training and best practices
  • 24/7 remote monitoring setup
  • Scheduled maintenance plans
  • Ongoing customer support and reorders
