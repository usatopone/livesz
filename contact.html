Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum order quantity?

We don't have a minimum order size. We design solar plants tailored to your specific energy needs, from small residential systems to large agricultural operations.

How long does production take?

A standard residential installation typically takes 1-3 days. Larger farm or custom projects vary, but we provide a clear timeline during the consultation phase.

Do you provide samples?

We provide a detailed system design proposal, including performance estimates and equipment specifications, before you commit to a project.

What countries do you ship to?

We currently serve customers across the nation. Please contact us to confirm service availability in your specific area.
